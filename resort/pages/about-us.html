About Magnaga Water Beach Resort

Magnaga Waters Beach Resort provides a range services and amenities to help make your stay more comfortable. Never be out of touch with your contacts, with free Wi-Fi offered throughout your stay. If you plan to arrive by car, you'll appreciate the resort's available parking, right on-site. For lazy days and nights, in-room conveniences like daily housekeeping let you make the most of your room.

Feel right at home during your stay at Magnaga Waters Beach Resort. Some rooms are equipped with linen service and air conditioning for your convenience. Some rooms at Magnaga Waters Beach Resort include special design features like a balcony or terrace. In-room entertainment such as television and cable TV is provided for guests in some selected rooms. The resort also offers a refrigerator, bottled water and instant tea in some rooms for when you feel like it is needed. Knowing that bathroom amenities play an important role in increasing guests' satisfaction, the resort provides a hair dryer, toiletries, bathrobes and towels in some select rooms.
